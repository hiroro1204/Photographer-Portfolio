About

Theo Lume is an international photographer known for capturing the subtle interplay of light, shadow, and atmosphere. With a strong foundation in portraiture, street photography, landscapes, and editorial work, his images reveal a timeless quality—blending raw authenticity with a refined artistic vision.
Driven by a fascination with fleeting moments, Theo often works with natural light, creating photographs that feel both cinematic and intimate. His style merges film-like textures with modern sensibilities, evoking nostalgia while remaining contemporary.

His work has attracted a diverse range of clients—from fashion brands and editorial magazines to architects and cultural organizations—while also resonating deeply with audiences on a personal level. Whether documenting the quiet beauty of urban life or the vastness of natural landscapes, Theo Lume seeks to tell stories that transcend borders and speak to universal human experience.
